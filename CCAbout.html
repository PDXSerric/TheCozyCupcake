Our Story

The Cozy Cupcake began with two friends, Gemma and Brittany, who bonded over their shared love of baking. What started as a casual hobby of creating delicious cupcakes quickly turned into something more. The two spent countless hours experimenting with flavors, decorating designs, and perfecting their recipes. As their passion for baking grew, so did their dream of sharing their creations with the world.

What began with just cupcakes soon expanded into an array of sweet treats. From cakes for every occasion to freshly baked pastries and melt-in-your-mouth cookies, The Cozy Cupcake blossomed into a bakery that offers something for everyone. With each new creation, Gemma and Brittany bring their love of baking to life, always striving to deliver the most delicious and beautiful desserts. What started as a simple friendship has grown into a thriving business, fueled by their passion for crafting the perfect baked goods to share with others.
